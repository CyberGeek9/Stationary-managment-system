WELCOME TO OUR STATIONARY MANAGEMENT SYSTEM

The Stationary Management System is a state-of-the-art software developed by the Punjab Information Technology Board (PITB) to streamline the management of office supplies. This user-friendly platform is designed to ensure efficient tracking of stationary items, from inventory management to usage monitoring. The system provides a seamless experience for organizations, helping them maintain optimal stock levels, reduce wastage, and improve overall operational efficiency.
